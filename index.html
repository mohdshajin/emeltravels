Dubai

Dubai is a city and emirate in the United Arab Emirates known for luxury shopping, ultramodern architecture and a lively nightlife scene. Burj Khalifa, an 830m-tall tower, dominates the skyscraper-filled skyline.

Indonesia

Indonesia consists of over 17,000 islands, including Sumatra, Java, Sulawesi, and parts of Borneo and New Guinea. Indonesia offers an adventure and attractions for everyone, from exploring ancient temples and hiking active volcanoes to diving in largely untouched waters.

Maldives

Maldives, is an archipelagic state located in South Asia, situated in the Indian Ocean. Maldives is a great destination for aqua lovers to try out different water activities, recommended for history lovers, for a great experience. Enamoring beaches, azure waters, thrilling activities, and exotic weather, Maldives tourism is adorned by such charming features and a lot more.

Azerbaijan

Azerbaijan has different regions each having their own attractions and specialties. Baku is the most famous tourist attractions. The Walled City of Baku combines elements of medieval and traditional Islamic Azerbaijan styles. It's famous for its epic mountain ranges and the mulberry groves and vineyards that flourish in its valleys.
